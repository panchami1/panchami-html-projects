by Harish Varma

The portra of Yashoda Rani with Shree Krishna was painted in 2009 by the Indian Neoclasiccal artist Harish, and today hangs in the Nasik. It is the third of three portraits of the Varma family the artist painted that year.

Date: 2009
Medium: Oil on Canvas
Dimensions: 100 x 70 cm
